FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you want to know before your first visit.

How long does a physiotherapy session take?
+

A typical session at Physio Pharma lasts 45–60 minutes. The first consultation may take up to 75 minutes as it includes a thorough assessment and history taking. Your therapist will give you a clear time estimate for your specific program.

What are your treatment fees?
+

We offer highly competitive and transparent pricing. Session fees vary based on the type of treatment. Please call us or WhatsApp for a detailed fee schedule. We also offer treatment packages at discounted rates for ongoing care.

Do I need a doctor's referral to visit?
+

No referral is needed! You can visit Physio Pharma directly. Our physiotherapists are qualified to assess and diagnose musculoskeletal conditions independently. However, if you have one from your doctor, it's helpful to bring it along.

How many sessions will I need for recovery?
+

Recovery time varies by condition. Acute injuries may need 6–10 sessions, while chronic conditions may require 15–20 sessions. After your initial assessment, your therapist will give a realistic treatment plan and timeline specific to you.

Do you offer home visit physiotherapy?
+

Yes, we offer home visit physiotherapy for patients who are unable to travel due to severe pain, post-surgery, elderly conditions, or disability. Please call or WhatsApp us to inquire about availability and charges.

Is physiotherapy painful?
+

Physiotherapy should not be painful in general. You may feel mild discomfort during some techniques, especially when working on injured areas, but our therapists always work within your pain tolerance and communicate with you throughout the session.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is available to answer any queries. Reach out to us directly — we respond within minutes!
